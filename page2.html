SUBSYSTEMS

Mission Analysis and Orbits

The role of Mission Analysis team is to design the entire layout of the space mission process. This includes the entire timeline of the mission, various budgets of the mission, and also the design and process constraints to be placed on other subsystems. It is also the role of Mission Analysis team to provide risk analysis of the said mission and design various alternatives inclusive of uncertainties that can happen during the mission.

AOCS

This concept completely focuses on important applications of the control process for spacecraft that is orbit of a spacecraft, attitude of a spacecraft, Position and Velocity quantities, Attitude and Attitude Rate quantities.

Propulsion

Selection and trade of a Propulsion system suitable for the mission. It includes propellant budgeting, Lift-Off mass estimation for the mission and selection of launch vehicle based on the lift-off mass. PMD and pressurant tanks are also designed and a top-level feedline system architecture is developed.

Communications and OBDH

Communication subsystem deals with establishing communication with spacecraft and earth, transmitting and receiving data . OBDH also called as on board data handling deals with management of data and its storage via tracking, Telemetry and commanding

Power

The spacecraft designed for this Asteroid mining mission will have solar panels which converts sun's energy into electricity powering the spacecraft. This electricity will charge the batteries and stored which will be used when spacecraft is in eclipse mode.

Thermal

Spacecraft thermal control is integral to mission success. The process of thermal control for a
spacecraft involves managing the energy entering and leaving the spacecraft to ensure that the
components of the spacecraft remain within an acceptable temperature range. Spacecraft perform
optimally and have longer working lives when the temperature of their components remains within
these boundaries, often near the temperature at which they were fabricated.

Structures

Discussion of different materials used for spacecraft bus and structure. It includes orientation of different payloads within the spacecraft framework in compliance with the launcher fairing.

Environmental Analysis

This concept mainly focuses on the space environment and the environment near the asteroid and the environmental effects that a spacecraft is going to face when in space.

Mining

Discussion and critique of different mining operational architecture and selection of mining method.
